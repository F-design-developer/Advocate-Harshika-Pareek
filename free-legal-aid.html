What We Offer

Free Legal Aid for Women & Child Matters

At Advocate Harshika Pareek, we are committed to providing free legal aid and support to women and children who need legal protection, guidance, and access to justice. Our mission is to make legal assistance accessible to those facing difficult circumstances and unable to afford professional legal services.

Women's Legal Rights

Free legal assistance for women facing domestic violence, sexual harassment, abuse, property disputes, maintenance, divorce, and other matters affecting their rights and safety.

Child Protection Cases

Legal representation and support for children in POCSO cases, child welfare matters, custody disputes, and other situations where protection and justice are required.

Dowry Act Violations

Assistance for women facing dowry-related harassment, cruelty, exploitation, and legal issues under the Dowry Prohibition Act.

Domestic Violence Act

Comprehensive legal guidance and support for victims of domestic abuse seeking protection orders, safety, and justice.

Sexual Harassment Cases

Free consultation and legal support for women and children affected by workplace harassment, abuse, or any form of sexual misconduct.

Free Legal Consultation

Initial legal consultation at no cost to understand your situation, explain your legal rights, and guide you toward the best possible legal solution.

Our aim is to ensure that justice remains accessible to everyone, especially women and children facing difficult circumstances. If you or someone you know requires free legal assistance in any of the above areas, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Eligibility

Who Can Apply?

Our free legal aid program is available to women and children who meet the following criteria:

Women in Need
Women facing domestic violence, harassment, or financial hardship
Children & Minors
Children requiring legal protection or representation in sensitive matters
Economically Weaker Sections
Individuals from economically weaker backgrounds unable to afford legal fees
